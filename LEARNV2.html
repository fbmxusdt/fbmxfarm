Early players buy seeds cheaply. As adoption grows and more seeds are locked in plots, your seed holdings become worth 2× more in FBMX terms. You earn profit on harvest AND on price appreciation simultaneously.

🚀 FBMX Token Economics

Why the Token Price Will Rise

FBMX isn't just a game currency. It is the fuel for an economy that creates structural buying pressure on every single transaction.

🔒
Deposit-Only Entry — Every Player Buys FBMX First
To play, you must deposit FBMX. There is no free-to-play bypass. Every new player is a FBMX buyer. 1,000 players each depositing $50 = $50,000 in direct FBMX demand. At 10,000 players: $500,000 in locked FBMX inside the contract.
📉
Seeds Are Burned on Late Harvests — Deflationary Mechanic
Every late harvest burns seeds from the supply. Fewer seeds = higher bonding curve price per seed = more FBMX needed to buy the same number of seeds. Burn events are permanently visible on-chain, creating a scarcity narrative the market can observe and price in.

Marketplace Creates Real FBMX Velocity
Every marketplace purchase transfers coins (FBMX) from buyer to seller. Sellers withdraw to their wallet as FBMX. This creates a circular economy where active players continuously recirculate FBMX through the contract, maintaining constant on-chain transaction volume that signals ecosystem health to external investors.
🏆
Leaderboard Competition Locks Capital
Players competing for Top 50 seed and coin positions will NOT sell or withdraw — they'll buy more FBMX to plant more plots and accumulate more seeds. Competitive vanity metrics are one of the most powerful capital retention tools in gaming. Leaderboard players become long-term FBMX HODLers.
